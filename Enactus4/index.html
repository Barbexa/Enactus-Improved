Enactus at Bayero University Kano


At Bayero University Kano Enactus

We are dedicated to cultivating entrepreneurial leadership focused on creating sustainable social and environmental impact.
Our organization fosters an environment where students apply business principles to develop innovative solutions for community challenges.
Enactus aims to empower students to become impactful leaders by providing the platform to develop and implement sustainable solutions that address critical community needs. We focus on fostering innovation and achieving tangible, long-term results.

What We Do

A leadership and entrepreneurship student organization cultivating social entrepreneurs who create social impact by solving problems.
